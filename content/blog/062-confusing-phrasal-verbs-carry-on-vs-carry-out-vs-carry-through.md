# Confusing Phrasal Verbs: Carry on vs Carry out vs Carry through with Sentences & Hindi Meanings for Competitive Exams

In major competitive examinations such as SSC CGL, SBI PO, IBPS PO, UPSC CDS, and NDA, phrasal verbs based on the verb **"Carry"** often create a great deal of confusion. Candidates frequently struggle to differentiate between **"Carry on,"** **"Carry out,"** and **"Carry through."** Because these expressions look similar, examiners frequently use them in sentence improvement, error detection, and double-filler questions. 

To help you gain conceptual clarity, the academic editorial team at **kajubadamvocabulary.in** has put together this guide. Through our standard bilingual story approach, a comprehensive masterclass table, detailed grammatical spotlights, and a rigorous practice set, we break down these confusing structures so you can handle them confidently in your upcoming papers.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Carry on** | To continue doing something, or to behave in an excited/anxious way. | जारी रखना; या उत्तेजित होकर व्यवहार करना। | We must **carry on** our efforts despite the temporary economic downturn. | Often followed by 'with' plus a noun or the gerund form of a verb. |
| **Carry out** | To execute, perform, or conduct a task, order, or experiment. | आदेश का पालन करना, काम को पूरा करना या प्रयोग करना। | The military officers were ordered to **carry out** the evacuation plan immediately. | Highly transitive. | **Carry through** | To sustain someone or complete something successfully despite immense difficulties. | संकट में सहारा देना या कठिन परिस्थिति में किसी कार्य को अंत तक पूरा करना। | Her unwavering determination **carried her through** the most challenging phase of her career. | Can be transitive or separable. The object is often a person being sustained. |

| **Carry off** | To win a prize, or to handle a difficult situation successfully. | पुरस्कार जीतना या किसी कठिन परिस्थिति को सफलतापूर्वक संभाल लेना। | She was nervous about the presentation, but she **carried it off** with great confidence. | Distinct from \"carry out.\" \"Carry off\" emphasizes performance, flair, or winning. |
| **Carry forward** | To transfer a cumulative total, an idea, or a legacy to a future stage or page. | अगले पृष्ठ या चरण पर ले जाना; आगे बढ़ाना। | The accounting department needs to **carry forward** the net balance to the new ledger. | Commonly used in financial accounting and legacy-building contexts. |
| **Carry back** | To cause someone to remember a past time or experience. | अतीत की याद दिलाना। | Hearing that old melody always **carries me back** to my childhood days in the village. | Transitive and separable. Typically takes a personal pronoun as a direct object. |

## 🔍 Advanced Grammar Spotlights — व्याकरण के मुख्य नियम

### ⚡ Spotlight 1: Pronoun Separation Constraint with "Carry" Phrasal Verbs
In sentence-correction and error-spotting exercises, candidates often struggle with the correct placement of personal pronouns when dealing with transitive, separable phrasal verbs like **"carry out"** and **"carry off."**

*   **The Rule:** If the direct object of a separable phrasal verb is a personal pronoun (such as *it, him, her, us, them*), the pronoun **must** be placed between the verb and the prepositional particle. Placing the pronoun at the very end is grammatically incorrect. Conversely, if the object is a noun, it can be placed either in the middle or at the very end.
*   ❌ **Incorrect:** The manager assigned the tasks and the junior staff carried out them.
*   ✅ **Correct:** The manager assigned the tasks and the junior staff **carried them out**.
*   ✅ **Correct:** The junior staff carried out **the tasks**. (Noun object can go at the end)

### ⚡ Spotlight 2: Structural Contrast — "Carry through" vs. "Carry out"
Examiners frequently swap **"carry out"** and **"carry through"** to test a candidate's understanding of sentence structures.

*   **The Rule:** 
    1.  **Carry out** refers to the execution of a structured plan, order, experiment, or duty. The object is typically an abstract noun representing a task (e.g., *instructions, orders, surveys, research*).
        *   *Example:* They **carried out** a detailed survey. (Correct)
    2.  **Carry through** means to sustain or support a person during a crisis, or to complete a difficult commitment until the very end. When used in the sense of "sustaining," it takes a **person** as its direct object. When used in the sense of "completing," the object is usually a difficult commitment, pledge, or initiative.
        *   ❌ **Incorrect:** His deep optimism carried out him during the period of prolonged unemployment.
        *   ✅ **Correct:** His deep optimism **carried him through** the period of prolonged unemployment.

#### Q1. Spot the Error — Pronoun Placement Trap (SSC CGL Tier-2 Level)
**Question:** The research wing designed a new protocol (I) / to counter the digital threat, (II) / and the technical team successfully carried out it yesterday. (III)

**Options:**
(A) Only (I)
(B) Only (II)
(C) Only (III)
(D) Both (II) and (III)
(E) No Error

**Correct Answer - C**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The error is in part (III). The phrasal verb "carry out" is a transitive, separable phrasal verb. When the direct object is a pronoun (in this case, "it"), it must be placed between the verb "carried" and the particle "out." Therefore, "carried out it" must be corrected to **"carried it out."**
- **Hindi:** त्रुटि वाक्य के भाग (III) में है। "Carry out" एक सकर्मक (transitive) और विभाजनीय (separable) फ्रेज़ल वर्ब है। जब इसका ऑब्जेक्ट कोई प्रोनाउन (जैसे "it") होता है, तो उसे वर्ब (carried) और पार्टिकल (out) के बीच में रखा जाना अनिवार्य है। अतः "carried out it" को सुधारकर **"carried it out"** किया जाना चाहिए।

#### Q2. Fill in the Blank — Dual Phrasal Verb Choice (Bank PO Level)
**Question:** While the general manager wanted to ________ the strategic expansion plan despite the low budget, the board warned him not to get ________ by unrealistic growth projections.

**Options:**
(A) carry on / carried through
(B) carry through / carried off
(C) carry out / carried away
(D) carry off / carried over
(E) carry over / carried on

**Correct Answer - C**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The first blank requires a phrasal verb meaning to execute or perform a plan, which is "carry out." The second blank requires a phrasal verb indicating that the manager should not lose his self-control or become over-excited by unrealistic projections, which is "carried away." This makes option (C) the only logically and grammatically correct option.
- **Hindi:** पहले रिक्त स्थान में योजना को "क्रियान्वित करने या पूरा करने" के अर्थ में "carry out" की आवश्यकता है। दूसरे रिक्त स्थान में अवास्तविक अनुमानों से "उत्तेजित होकर नियंत्रण खोने या बह जाने" के अर्थ में "carried away" की आवश्यकता है। अतः विकल्प (C) अर्थ और व्याकरण दोनों की दृष्टि से सबसे उपयुक्त है।

#### Q3. Phrase Replacement — Survival Context (UPSC CDS Level)
**Question:** The stranded sailors had very little drinking water, but their strong willpower **carried them off** until the rescue helicopters arrived.

**Options:**
(A) carried them through
(B) carried them out
(C) carried them over
(D) carried them on
(E) No correction required

**Correct Answer - A**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The context describes a difficult survival situation where the sailors were sustained or kept alive by their willpower. The phrasal verb "carry through" means to sustain someone through a crisis. "Carry off" means to win or handle a situation successfully, which is incorrect here. Therefore, **"carried them through"** is the correct replacement.
- **Hindi:** वाक्य का संदर्भ फंसे हुए नाविकों को उनकी इच्छाशक्ति द्वारा संकट के समय "सहारा देने या जीवित रखने" से है। किसी कठिन समय में सहारा देने के अर्थ में सही फ्रेज़ल वर्ब **"carry through"** होता है। "Carry off" का अर्थ जीतना या संभालना होता है, जो यहाँ अनुचित है। अतः विकल्प (A) सही उत्तर है।

#### Q4. Sentence Improvement — Transfer of Assets (NDA Level)
**Question:** The accountant confirmed that the unused credit balance would **carry on** to the next financial quarter automatically.

**Options:**
(A) carry through
(B) carry over
(C) carry off
(D) carry forward with
(E) No correction required

**Correct Answer - B**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The sentence refers to transferring a credit balance from one financial quarter to another. - **Hindi:** यहाँ अप्रयुक्त क्रेडिट बैलेंस (unused credit balance) को अगले वित्तीय तिमाही में "स्थानांतरित करने या आगे ले जाने" का संदर्भ है। किसी राशि या समय को आगे बढ़ाने/हस्तांतरित करने के अर्थ में सही फ्रेज़ल वर्ब **"carry over"** होता है। "Carry on" का अर्थ जारी रखना होता है, जो वित्तीय बैलेंस के लिए उपयुक्त नहीं है। अतः विकल्प (B) सही सुधार है।

#### Q5. Spot the Error — Syntactic Misuse of Carry Phrasal Verbs (Bank PO Level)
**Question:** Although the lead actor fell ill hours before the play, (I) / he managed to carry through the demanding role (II) / and received a standing ovation from the audience. (III)

**Options:**
(A) Only (I)
(B) Only (II)
(C) Only (III)
(D) Both (I) and (III)
(E) No Error

**Correct Answer - B**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The error lies in part (II). The context describes an actor successfully handling or performing a highly demanding role despite being sick. The correct phrasal verb meaning to handle a difficult performance or situation successfully is "carry off." "Carry through" would mean to sustain a person, which does not fit the active execution of a theatrical role here. Thus, "carry through" should be replaced with **"carry off."**
- **Hindi:** त्रुटि वाक्य के भाग (II) में है। यहाँ अभिनेता द्वारा बीमार होने के बावजूद एक कठिन भूमिका को "सफलतापूर्वक निभाने या मंचित करने" का संदर्भ है। किसी कठिन कार्य या प्रदर्शन को सफलतापूर्वक संभाल लेने के अर्थ में सही फ्रेज़ल वर्ब **"carry off"** होता है। "Carry through" का अर्थ सहारा देना होता है। अतः यहाँ "carry through" को बदलकर **"carry off"** किया जाना चाहिए।

#### Q6. Fill in the Blank — Execution vs. Continuance (SSC CHSL Level)
**Question:** The chief investigator advised the officers to ________ with the questioning until the suspect disclosed where the stolen documents were hidden.

**Options:**
(A) carry out
(B) carry on
(C) carry off
(D) carry over
(E) carry back

**Correct Answer - B**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The context indicates continuing an activity (questioning the suspect). - **Hindi:** यहाँ पूछताछ की प्रक्रिया को "जारी रखने" का संदर्भ है। किसी गतिविधि को जारी रखने के अर्थ में सही फ्रेज़ल वर्ब **"carry on"** होता है, जिसके बाद प्रायः "with" का प्रयोग होता है। "Carry out" का अर्थ पूरा करना होता है, लेकिन "carry out with" व्याकरण सम्मत नहीं है। अतः विकल्प (B) सही उत्तर है।

#### Q7. Spot the Error — Intransitive Passive Confusion (UPSC CDS Level)
**Question:** The administrative committee was carried away by (I) / the emotional speeches, but they quickly realized that (II) / the unresolved disputes would carry out into the next session. (III)

**Options:**
(A) Only (I)
(B) Only (II)
(C) Only (III)
(D) Both (II) and (III)
(E) No Error

**Correct Answer - C**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The error is in part (III). The sentence discusses unresolved disputes transferring or extending into the next session. The correct phrasal verb for transferring to another time or period is "carry over." "Carry out" means to execute a task, which is completely illogical in this context. Therefore, "carry out" should be changed to **"carry over."**
- **Hindi:** त्रुटि वाक्य के भाग (III) में है। यहाँ अनसुलझे विवादों के अगले सत्र में "स्थानांतरित होने या खिंच जाने" का संदर्भ है। समय या विषय के अगले चरण में जाने के लिए सही फ्रेज़ल वर्ब **"carry over"** होता है। "Carry out" का अर्थ किसी योजना को लागू करना होता है, जो यहाँ निरर्थक है। अतः विकल्प (C) सही उत्तर है।

#### Q10. Identification of Grammatically Correct Sentences (SBI PO Mains Level)
**Question:** Read the following sentences carefully and identify which of them are grammatically correct:
(I) The surgeons had to carry out the complex procedure under dim emergency lights.
(II) Although the team lost their primary funding, their sheer passion carried them through.
(III) We need to carry forward the outstanding balance of this month into the new ledger.

**Options:**
(A) Only (I)
(B) Only (II)
(C) Both (I) and (III)
(D) All (I), (II), and (III)
(E) None of the sentences are correct

**Correct Answer - D**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** All three sentences are grammatically and contextually correct. Sentence (I) correctly uses "carry out" to mean performing a medical procedure. Sentence (II) correctly uses "carried them through" to mean sustaining the team during a crisis. Sentence (III) correctly uses "carry forward" to mean transferring a financial balance to the next ledger.
- **Hindi:** दिए गए तीनों वाक्य व्याकरण और संदर्भ की दृष्टि से पूरी तरह से शुद्ध हैं। वाक्य (I) में चिकित्सा प्रक्रिया को "पूरा करने" के लिए "carry out" का सही प्रयोग हुआ है। वाक्य (II) में संकट के समय टीम को "सहारा देने" के अर्थ में "carried them through" का सही प्रयोग हुआ है। वाक्य (III) में वित्तीय शेष राशि को "आगे बढ़ाने" के लिए "carry forward" का सही प्रयोग हुआ है। अतः विकल्प (D) सही उत्तर है।

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1. "Carry out" और "Carry through" के बीच मुख्य अंतर क्या है?
**Answer:** "Carry out" का प्रयोग किसी काम, आदेश, नियम, योजना या परीक्षण को **पूरा करने या क्रियान्वित करने** (execute/perform) के लिए किया जाता है। इसके विपरीत, "Carry through" का प्रयोग किसी कठिन परिस्थिति या संकट में किसी व्यक्ति को **सहारा देने** (sustain/support) या किसी कठिन वादे/प्रतिबद्धता को **अंत तक पूरा करने** के लिए किया जाता है।

### Q2. "Carry away" का प्रयोग आमतौर पर पैसिव वॉइस में ही क्यों किया जाता है?
**Answer:** "Carry away" का अर्थ होता है अत्यधिक उत्साह, खुशी या भावनाओं के कारण अपना नियंत्रण खो देना (to lose self-control)। चूंकि यह स्थिति किसी बाहरी प्रभाव या भावना के वश में होने से उत्पन्न होती है, इसलिए इसे प्रायः पैसिव फॉर्म (जैसे: *She was carried away by the music* या *Don't get carried away*) में लिखा जाता है।

### Q3. वित्तीय संदर्भों में "Carry over" और "Carry forward" में क्या अंतर होता है?
**Answer:** लेखांकन (Accounting) में दोनों काफी हद तक समान हैं, लेकिन सूक्ष्म अंतर यह है:
*   **Carry over:** इसका अर्थ है किसी अप्रयुक्त चीज़ (जैसे बची हुई छुट्टियाँ, टैक्स क्रेडिट) को अगले वर्ष या अवधि में **स्थानांतरित करना**।
*   **Carry forward:** इसका अर्थ है किसी वर्तमान पृष्ठ या खाते के कुल योग (total balance) को अगले पृष्ठ या नए बही-खाते (ledger) पर **आगे ले जाना**।
