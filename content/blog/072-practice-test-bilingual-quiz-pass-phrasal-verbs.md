# Practice Test & Bilingual Quiz: Test Your Knowledge on 'Pass' Phrasal Verbs for Exams

Active recall and strategic practice are highly effective methods for retaining complex vocabulary. In major competitive exams like SSC CGL, SBI PO, IBPS PO, NDA, and CDS, phrasal verbs built around the verb **"Pass"** are frequently tested. Because these expressions (such as *pass out, pass off, pass over,* and *pass up*) look similar, candidates often make errors when choosing their answers under exam pressure.

Understanding the difference between a project being "passed off" successfully and an individual being "passed over" for a promotion is crucial for securing high marks. ## 📝 The Core Vocabulary Masterclass — मुख्य शब्दकोश तालिका

This table breaks down the syntax, meanings, and common exam traps of the eight key phrasal verbs with **"Pass"** tested in recent exams.

| Phrasal Verb | English Meaning | Hindi Meaning | Exam-Style Example | Grammar Trap / Key Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pass down** | To hand down or transmit traditions, knowledge, or property. | परंपरा, ज्ञान या संपत्ति को अगली पीढ़ी को सौंपना या हस्तांतरित करना। | These ancient recipes have been **passed down** through generations. | Commonly used in the passive voice to describe generational heritage. |
| **Pass away** | To die (used as a polite or formal euphemism). | मृत्यु होना या स्वर्गवास होना (विनम्र संदर्भ में)। | The veteran actor **passed away** peacefully at his residence yesterday. | Strictly intransitive; cannot be used in passive voice constructions. |
| **Pass by** | To go past a place or person; or to ignore/overlook an opportunity. | बिना रुके पास से गुजरना; या किसी अवसर को अनदेखा करना। | Do not let this wonderful career opportunity **pass you by**. | Often used in the active form to describe an opportunity slipping away. |
| **Pass up** | To decline or reject a good offer or opportunity. | किसी अच्छे अवसर या प्रस्ताव को अस्वीकार करना या छोड़ देना। | He decided to **pass up** the job offer because the salary was too low. | Transitive; requires a direct object representing an opportunity. |
| **Pass out** | To faint or lose consciousness; or to graduate from an academy. | बेहोश होना; या किसी अकादमी से प्रशिक्षण पूरा कर दीक्षित होना। | She felt extremely dizzy due to the heat and nearly **passed out**. | Has two completely distinct meanings depending on clinical or military contexts. |
| **Pass off** | To happen or take place in a specific way; or to misrepresent. | विशेष तरीके से संपन्न होना; या किसी चीज़ का ढोंग/गलत रूप से पेश करना। | Despite the initial security threats, the concert **passed off** smoothly. | Polysemous. Takes a reflexive pronoun when meaning to impersonate someone. |
| **Pass on** | To transmit information/disease; or to die (polite alternative). | जानकारी/बीमारी फैलाना; या स्वर्गवासी होना (विनम्र संदर्भ में)। | Please **pass on** this urgent message to all the department heads. | Can be transitive (transmitting things) or intransitive (meaning to die). |
| **Pass over** | To ignore, overlook, or bypass someone for a promotion or job. | पदोन्नति या नौकरी के लिए किसी को नजरअंदाज करना या अनदेखा करना। | She was **passed over** for the senior role in favor of an external candidate. | Transitive and separable; the ignored person is placed in the middle. |

## 🔍 Advanced Grammar Spotlights — व्याकरण के मुख्य नियम

### ⚡ Spotlight 1: Prepositional Collisions — "Pass over" vs. "Pass up" vs. "Pass on"
Choosing between **"Pass over,"** **"Pass up,"** and **"Pass on"** is a frequent source of errors in advanced multiple-choice questions.

*   **The Rule:** 
    1.  **Pass over** is transitive and focuses on a selection committee or manager deliberately ignoring or bypassing an individual for a promotion, title, or job.
        *   *Example:* No talented applicant should be **passed over** during the selection process.
    2.  **Pass up** is also transitive but refers to the subject willingly declining or rejecting a beneficial offer, opportunity, or invitation.
        *   *Example:* I decided to **pass up** the invitation because of my busy schedule.
    3.  **Pass on** can be transitive (meaning to transfer an object or transmit information) or intransitive (serving as a polite alternative to *pass away*).
        *   *Example:* Please **pass the report on** to the regional director.

### ⚡ Spotlight 2: Active Voice Constraint of "Pass off" (Happen) vs. "Pass off as" (Pretend)
Examiners frequently use the voice and reflexive pronoun requirements of **"Pass off"** to construct tricky grammar questions.

*   **The Rule:** 
    1.  When **Pass off** means "to take place or happen in a specific way," it is strictly **intransitive and active**. You cannot write this construction in the passive voice.
        *   ❌ **Incorrect:** The annual general meeting *was passed off* smoothly yesterday.
        *   ✅ **Correct:** The annual general meeting **passed off** smoothly yesterday.
    2.  When **Pass off as** means "to misrepresent or pretend to be someone else," it is **transitive and separable**. It requires a reflexive pronoun (like *himself, herself, themselves*) placed between "pass" and "off."
        *   *Example:* The con artist managed to **pass himself off** as a certified wealth manager.

#### Q1. Spot the Error — Semantic Word Selection Trap (SSC CGL Tier-2 Level)
**Question:** During the rigorous military drill (I) / in the humid jungle environment, (II) / several trainees passed over due to exhaustion. (III)

**Options:**
(A) Only (I)
(B) Only (II)
(C) Only (III)
(D) Both (II) and (III)
(E) No Error

**Correct Answer - C**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The error lies in part (III). The phrasal verb meaning to lose consciousness or faint is "pass out," not "pass over." "Pass over" means to ignore or bypass someone for a promotion. Therefore, "passed over" must be replaced with "passed out" to restore the correct meaning of the sentence.
- **Hindi:** त्रुटि वाक्य के भाग (III) में है। शारीरिक थकान के कारण बेहोश होने या चक्कर आने के अर्थ में सही फ्रेज़ल वर्ब "pass out" (भूतकाल "passed out") होता है, न कि "pass over"। "Pass over" का अर्थ किसी पदोन्नति के लिए नजरअंदाज करना होता है। अतः वाक्य को शुद्ध करने के लिए "passed over" के स्थान पर "passed out" का प्रयोग होना चाहिए।

**Question:** The entire community fell into silent mourning when the beloved village elder ________ last week, but their spirits lifted when the village's first female candidate ________ from the administrative academy.

**Options:**
(A) passed off / passed over
(B) passed away / passed out
(C) passed out / passed away
(D) passed down / passed up
(E) passed up / passed on

**Correct Answer - B**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The first blank describes the death of the village elder, which is represented by the polite phrasal verb "passed away." The second blank describes the female candidate graduating from the administrative academy, which is represented by the phrasal verb "passed out." This makes option (B) the only contextually and grammatically correct option.
- **Hindi:** पहले रिक्त स्थान में ग्राम बुजुर्ग की मृत्यु को दर्शाने के लिए "passed away" की आवश्यकता है। दूसरे रिक्त स्थान में महिला उम्मीदवार के प्रशासनिक अकादमी से "दीक्षित होने/सफलतापूर्वक कोर्स पूरा करने" के अर्थ में "passed out" की आवश्यकता है। अतः विकल्प (B) सही और तर्कसंगत उत्तर है।

#### Q3. Sentence Improvement — Ignored Promotion Context (UPSC CDS Level)
**Question:** The research head felt deeply disappointed when she was **passed up** for the global innovation award despite her path-breaking discoveries.

**Options:**
(A) passed off
(B) passed over
(C) passed out
(D) passed down
(E) No correction required

**Correct Answer - B**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The sentence describes a research head being overlooked or ignored for a prestigious award. The correct phrasal verb meaning to ignore, bypass, or overlook someone for a promotion or award is "pass over" (past tense "passed over"). "Passed up" means to decline an opportunity, which is incorrect because she did not decline it; she was bypassed. Thus, option (B) is the correct improvement.
- **Hindi:** यहाँ उत्कृष्ट खोजों के बावजूद अनुसंधान प्रमुख (research head) को पुरस्कार के लिए "नजरअंदाज किए जाने" का संदर्भ है। पुरस्कार या पदोन्नति के लिए नजरअंदाज किए जाने के अर्थ में सही फ्रेज़ल वर्ब **"pass over"** होता है। "Passed up" का अर्थ स्वयं किसी अवसर को अस्वीकार करना होता है। अतः विकल्प (B) सही सुधार है।

#### Q4. Phrase Replacement — Fraudulent Pretense Context (NDA Level)
**Question:** The undercover operative managed to **pass over** himself as a foreign merchant to gather critical intelligence behind enemy lines.

**Options:**
(A) pass off
(B) pass out
(C) pass up
(D) pass down
(E) No correction required

**Correct Answer - A**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The sentence describes an operative pretending to be or misrepresenting himself as a foreign merchant. The correct phrasal verb meaning to pretend to be someone else or to misrepresent is "pass off" (specifically, "pass oneself off as"). "Pass over" means to ignore for a promotion, which does not fit. Therefore, option (A) is the correct replacement.
- **Hindi:** वाक्य में एक गुप्त एजेंट (operative) द्वारा विदेशी व्यापारी के रूप में "गलत रूप से पेश करने या ढोंग करने" का संदर्भ है। किसी अन्य व्यक्ति के रूप में ढोंग करने के लिए सही फ्रेज़ल वर्ब **"pass off"** (pass oneself off as) होता है। "Pass over" का अर्थ नजरअंदाज करना होता है, जो यहाँ असंगत है। अतः विकल्प (A) सही सुधार है।

#### Q5. Spot the Error — Intransitive Verb Trait (Bank PO Level)
**Question:** The legendary author was passed away peacefully (I) / at his mountain cabin (II) / after completing his final autobiographical masterpiece. (III)

**Options:**
(A) Only (I)
(B) Only (II)
(C) Only (III)
(D) Both (I) and (III)
(E) No Error

**Correct Answer - A**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The error lies in part (I). The phrasal verb "pass away" meaning to die is strictly intransitive. Since intransitive phrasal verbs cannot be used in the passive voice, using the auxiliary verb "was" before "passed away" is grammatically incorrect. The sentence should be written in the active past tense as "The legendary author passed away peacefully..."
- **Hindi:** त्रुटि वाक्य के भाग (I) में है। "Pass away" (मृत्यु होना) एक अकर्मक क्रिया (intransitive verb) है। अकर्मक क्रियाओं का पैसिव वॉइस नहीं बनाया जा सकता, इसलिए "passed away" के पहले सहायक क्रिया "was" का प्रयोग व्याकरणिक रूप से पूरी तरह गलत है। इसे सुधारकर एक्टिव वॉइस में केवल **"passed away"** लिखा जाना चाहिए। अतः विकल्प (A) सही उत्तर है।

#### Q10. Comprehensive Syntax Check — Statement Evaluation (SBI PO Mains Level)
**Question:** Identify the grammatically correct sentence(s) from the options provided below:
(I) The cultural exhibition passed off successfully despite the initial power outage.
(II) She was overwhelmed with emotion and passed out upon hearing the good news.
(III) No capable candidate should be passed over during the selection process.

**Options:**
(A) Only (I)
(B) Only (II)
(C) Both (I) and (III)
(D) All (I), (II), and (III)
(E) None of the sentences are correct

**Correct Answer - D**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** All three sentences are grammatically and syntactically correct. Sentence (I) correctly uses "passed off" to mean took place or happened. Sentence (II) correctly uses "passed out" to mean fainted due to overwhelming emotion. Sentence (III) correctly uses "passed over" to mean bypassed or ignored for a position.
- **Hindi:** दिए गए तीनों वाक्य व्याकरण और संदर्भ की दृष्टि से पूरी तरह से शुद्ध हैं। वाक्य (I) में प्रदर्शनी के "संपन्न होने" के अर्थ में "passed off" का सही प्रयोग हुआ है। वाक्य (II) में अच्छी खबर सुनकर "बेहोश होने" के अर्थ में "passed out" का सही प्रयोग हुआ है। वाक्य (III) में उम्मीदवारों को चयन प्रक्रिया में "नजरअंदाज करने" के अर्थ में "passed over" का सही प्रयोग हुआ है। अतः विकल्प (D) सही उत्तर है।

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1. "Pass over" और "Pass up" के बीच मुख्य अंतर क्या है?
**Answer:** इन दोनों के बीच अंतर को उनके संदर्भ से समझा जा सकता है:
*   **Pass over:** इसका प्रयोग तब होता है जब किसी व्यक्ति को किसी नौकरी या पदोन्नति (promotion) के लिए **नजरअंदाज या बाईपास** कर दिया जाता है (जैसे: *He was passed over for the job*).
*   **Pass up:** इसका प्रयोग तब होता है जब आप स्वयं किसी अच्छे अवसर या प्रस्ताव को **अस्वीकार कर देते हैं या छोड़ देते हैं** (जैसे: *Never pass up a good opportunity*).

### Q2. "He tried to pass over himself as a doctor" लिखना गलत क्यों है?
**Answer:** यहाँ "डॉक्टर के रूप में खुद को गलत तरीके से पेश करने या ढोंग करने" का संदर्भ है। इस विशिष्ट अर्थ के लिए सही फ्रेज़ल वर्ब **"pass off"** (pass oneself off as) होता है, न कि "pass over"। अतः सही वाक्य **"He tried to pass himself off as a doctor"** होगा।

### Q3. क्या "Pass out" का प्रयोग पैसिव वॉइस (Passive Voice) में किया जा सकता है?
**Answer:** नहीं, "Pass out" (बेहोश होना या दीक्षित होना) एक अकर्मक क्रिया (intransitive phrasal verb) है। चूंकि अकर्मक क्रियाओं का कोई प्रत्यक्ष कर्म (direct object) नहीं होता, इसलिए व्याकरण के नियमों के अनुसार इनका पैसिव वॉइस बनाना पूरी तरह अशुद्ध माना जाता है।
