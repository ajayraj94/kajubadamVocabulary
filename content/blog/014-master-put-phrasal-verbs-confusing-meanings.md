# Master 'Put' Phrasal Verbs: Confusing Meanings Explained with Hindi Translation

In advanced competitive examinations like **SSC CGL Tier 2**, **IBPS PO**, and **SBI PO Mains**, vocabulary questions are not always about testing rare words. Often, examiners focus on high-yield, common verbs that carry multiple, highly confusing meanings depending on the context. Within this category, phrasal verbs with **"Put"** are arguably the most prolific and deceptively complex. 

For instance, the phrasal verb "put up" can mean physically building a wall, providing a place for a guest to sleep, or putting up a fight. Misinterpreting these polysemous variations in a reading comprehension passage or close test can lead to costly mistakes. ## 🔍 Advanced Grammar Spotlights — व्याकरण के मुख्य नियम

### ⚡ Spotlight 1: Polysemy Confusion with "Put up" — Build vs. Lodge
In competitive exams, **"put up"** is heavily targeted because its meaning shifts completely based on transitively attached nouns.

1.  **To Erect/Build (Transitive):** When followed by a building, tent, or structure, it means to construct.
    *   *Example:* The construction crew decided to **put up** a temporary barrier.
2.  **To Accommodate/Lodge (Transitive Separable):** When followed by a person, it means to host them at your home.
    *   *Example:* If you visit New Delhi, I can **put** you **up** for the night. (Correct)
    *   ❌ *Incorrect:* If you visit New Delhi, I can put up you for the night. (Pronoun must go in the middle).
3.  **To Tolerate (With "with"):** Adding "with" completely shifts the meaning to endurance. Do not confuse "put up" (accommodate) with "put up with" (tolerate).

### ⚡ Spotlight 2: Pronoun Placement Constraints in Separable "Put forward" and "Put back"
A common error in error spotting sections is the incorrect placement of pronoun objects within multi-word phrasal verbs.

*   **Rule:** Both "put forward" and "put back" are transitive separable phrasal verbs. When the object is a pronoun (like *it*, *them*, *him*, *her*), the pronoun must be placed between the verb "put" and the particle (forward/back).
*   ❌ **Incorrect:** The committee reviewed the proposal and decided to put forward it during the general assembly.
*   ✅ **Correct:** The committee reviewed the proposal and decided to **put it forward** during the general assembly.

#### Q1. Fill in the Blank — Accommodation Context
**Question:** Since my flight was delayed by twelve hours, the airline cooperative kindly offered to ________ me in a nearby transit hotel.

**Options:**
(A) put out
(B) put forward
(C) put up
(D) put down
(E) put off

**Correct Answer - C**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The sentence describes the airline offering lodging or accommodation because of a flight delay. The phrasal verb "put up" means to provide accommodation or lodge someone, which fits the context. Note that the pronoun "me" is correctly placed in the middle ("put me up").
- **Hindi:** यह वाक्य उड़ान में देरी के कारण एयरलाइन द्वारा किसी को ट्रांजिट होटल में रहने या ठहराने की व्यवस्था करने के बारे में है। किसी को रहने की जगह देने या ठहराने के संदर्भ में सही फ्रेज़ल वर्ब "put up" होता है। ध्यान दें कि सर्वनाम "me" को सही ढंग से बीच में रखा गया है ("put me up")।

#### Q3. Spot the Error — Pronoun with "Put back"
**Question:** The librarian asked the student (I) / to take the historical manuscripts (II) / and put back them on the restricted shelf. (III)

**Options:**
(A) Only (I)
(B) Only (II)
(C) Only (III)
(D) Both (II) and (III)
(E) No Error

**Correct Answer - C**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The error is in part (III). "Put back" (meaning to return something to its original place) is a transitive separable phrasal verb. When the object is a pronoun (here, "them" referring to manuscripts), it must be placed between the verb and the particle. Therefore, "put back them" must be changed to **"put them back."**
- **Hindi:** त्रुटि वाक्य के भाग (III) में है। किसी वस्तु को उसके मूल स्थान पर वापस रखने के अर्थ में "Put back" एक separable (विभाजित होने वाला) फ्रेज़ल वर्ब है। जब इसका ऑब्जेक्ट कोई सर्वनाम होता है (यहाँ पांडुलिपियों के लिए "them"), तो उस सर्वनाम को हमेशा वर्ब और पार्टिकल के बीच में रखा जाना चाहिए। इसलिए, "put back them" को बदलकर **"put them back"** किया जाना चाहिए।

#### Q4. Spot the Error — Semantic Nuance of "Put out"
**Question:** We arrived so unannounced (I) / at the manager's residence that (II) / we feared we might have put out him. (III)

**Options:**
(A) Only (I)
(B) Only (II)
(C) Only (III)
(D) Both (I) and (III)
(E) No Error

**Correct Answer - C**
**Detailed Explanation:**
- **English:** The error is in part (III). The sentence discusses causing inconvenience or annoyance to the manager by arriving unannounced. While the phrasal verb "put out" is correct for this context, it is a separable transitive phrasal verb. Since the object is a pronoun ("him"), it must go in the middle, making the correct structure **"put him out."**
- **Hindi:** त्रुटि वाक्य के भाग (III) में है। बिना बताए घर पहुँचने से मैनेजर को असुविधा या परेशानी होने का संदर्भ है। "Put out" का अर्थ किसी को असुविधा में डालना होता है, लेकिन यह एक separable phrasal verb है। चूंकि ऑब्जेक्ट एक सर्वनाम ("him") है, इसलिए इसे बीच में होना चाहिए, जिससे सही संरचना **"put him out"** बनेगी।

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1. "Put up" के 'निर्माण करना' और 'रहने की जगह देना' इन दोनों अर्थों को वाक्य में कैसे समझें?
**Answer:** "Put up" का अर्थ वाक्य के ऑब्जेक्ट (संज्ञा) पर निर्भर करता है:
1. **निर्माण करना (Build):** यदि इसके बाद कोई निर्जीव ढांचा, इमारत, तंबू या दीवार हो (जैसे: *Put up a wall/tent*).
2. **रहने की जगह देना (Lodge/Accommodate):** यदि इसका ऑब्जेक्ट कोई व्यक्ति या सर्वनाम हो (जैसे: *Put me up / Put up the guests*).

### Q2. "Put off" के दो प्रमुख अर्थ कौन-से हैं जिन्हें परीक्षाओं में पूछा जाता है?
**Answer:** "Put off" के दो अत्यधिक महत्वपूर्ण अर्थ हैं:
1. **टालना या स्थगित करना (Postpone):** किसी कार्यक्रम को आगे बढ़ाना (जैसे: *The meeting was put off*).
2. **हतोत्साहित करना (Discourage):** किसी व्यवहार या चीज़ के कारण मन खट्टा होना या अरुचि होना (जैसे: *The bad smell put him off his food*).

### Q3. "Put down" के कितने अर्थ प्रतियोगी परीक्षाओं के लिए महत्वपूर्ण हैं?
**Answer:** "Put down" के तीन अर्थ सबसे ज्यादा परीक्षाओं में पूछे जाते हैं:
1. **दमन करना (Suppress):** किसी विद्रोह या प्रदर्शन को शांत करना (जैसे: *Put down the rebellion*).
2. **लिखना (Write):** नोट्स दर्ज करना (जैसे: *Put down the notes*).
3. **अपमानित करना (Humiliate):** किसी को नीचा दिखाना (जैसे: *Don't put down junior employees*).
